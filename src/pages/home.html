We are a small seminary dedicated to the Religo Romana, the religion of Ancient Rome.

Academic Programs

Religio Domestica

The religion of the home, for persons taking their first steps into the Relgio Romana. Courses include:

  • In-depth exploration of the rites and rituals for the house.
  • Discussion on the Gods and how to properly pay our respect.
    • the Lares
    • the Penates
    • the Genius
    • Juno
    • Vesta
  • An overview of the Religio Romana for the practitioner.
  • History of Rome and the Religo Romana.

Religio Romana

The state religion, for persons looking for a deeper dive into the religion. Certification from this course also provides membership in the Communio Templorum Romanorum, if you so choose.
